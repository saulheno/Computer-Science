General Info

TU857, formerly known as DT211 before our merge to TU Dublin, is a practical focused Computer Science course
with a heavy emphasis on practical work and real life use cases. The course previously took place
mostly on the Kevin Street and Aungier Street campus', but now is set to move to our new state of the art
Grangegorman campus.
