The Facilities

The college is spread across multiple Campus'. We are in the process
of moving almost everything to our new, state of the art Campus in Grangegorman
Due to the ongoing pandemic, learning is almost entirely remote, with a select few
attending labs and other practical classes, mostly in our old campus on Kevin Street.

Despite the setbacks with our move to Grangegorman, our facilities in our current Campus',
both Kevin and Aungier Street, are still modern and fully featured and will make for a great
tool for learning.
